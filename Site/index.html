About us

Formel Eins - A Destination for F1 fanatics

Formel Eins was foundeded in 2010 to be a place for former professional F1 racers to expres their passion for the sport and for new, avid racers-wannabe to receive training and experience. It had now expanded to a full pledged club bussiness with both professional sport teams and even people with only interests in following the sport. However, at its core, Formel Eins is a place for friendly interractions and conversations between F1 fans.

Formel Eins has been and will always thrive to provide the best experience for its members [...]
